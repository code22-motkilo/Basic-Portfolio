About Me

Michael McCluskey is a business analyst at Deloitte in McLean, VA. Michael specializes in software development lifecycle, project management and financial modeling. He is currently enrolled at George Washington University's Full-Stack Web Development course. He hopes to gain a well-rounded understanding of web development concepts as well as a solid foundation for learning more about programming. The development technologies learned in this course include Node.js, Angular.js, React.js, Java, SQL, MongoDB. By the end of the program, he will be ready to take on new responsibilities within his development team in both front-end and back-end In his free time, Michael enjoys playing soccer, traveling and recording music.
